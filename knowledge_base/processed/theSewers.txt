The Undercity Tunnels are a location in The Mines. It consists of a long series of Chambers, starting from the end of The Den and ending at The Barrier. There are six main Chambers that are located in The Undercity Tunnels, that being the Jeff Shop, The Railway Station, The Sewage Cells, The Sewage Pipes and the The Barrier.


Overview Appearance
The Undercity Tunnels appear to be a series of large, long Chambers with stone brick walls and dim green Radianceing. Many wooden pallets, boxes, closed barrels, and opened burning barrels are placed throughout the Undercity Tunnels. The Undercity Tunnels can generate multiple different room types, most commonly spawning as large, empty rectangular Chambers that can contain up to four Portals. Chambers with ladders surrounded in stone sewer-like tubes can also be found here. Almost all mines Chambers can show up throughout The Undercity Tunnels. Hiding spots such as Bolters, Grates and Dumpsters are all found in The Undercity Tunnels.

Door The Railway Station
Main article: The Railway Station
The Railway Station is a Conundrum room found in The Undercity Tunnels based around moving a minecart around a track until it reaches a section of planks bBolting the exit.

This room almost always has Mimic on the far door, but the correct door is always behind the boards.
The Participant must go up the ladder and pull the lever, then push the minecart until it reaches the end.

Door The Sewage Cells
Main article: The Sewage Cells
The Sewage Cells contain an extensive number of interconnected Chambers that form a maze-like structure. These Chambers include manholes, Sewer and Mines side Chambers, and medium-long tunnels. Most significantly, cell-like structures spawn frequently throughout. The location also contains a waterfall, which is guaranteed to spawn.

Door The Sewage Pipes
Main article: The Sewage Pipes
The Sewage Pipes begin with a long sewer corridor between Chambers 169 and 177. When the Participant reaches the end, the Queen Mutter will emerge from a hole in the ceiling behind them and roar at the Participant, only for Chase to appear from a hole punched in the wall, bBolting the Queen Mutter behind rubble in the process, before chasing the Participant.

4-Way Corridor
The 4-Way Corridor is the first and third room in the sewage pipes. It consists of an octagon shaped room with 3 possible exit Portals, with Guiding Radiance Radianceing up the correct door. This room is very similar to a type of room that can appear in the Hostel chases.
Two Tunnel Section
The Two Tunnel Section is the second room in the chase. It consists of a large room with two long tunnels at the end. The Queen Mutter will emerge from behind one of these tunnels, and force the Participant to go through the other tunnel.
Sewage Tunnels
The fourth and final room in the chase begins with a large, open room, with a sewer channel containing Chase's slime in the middle. Participants must run over wooden boards floating on Chase's slime to traverse the channel. A metal door grate bBolts the way into a series of smaller, twisting tunnels, which the Participant must break down by interacting with to proceed. The Guiding Radiance guides Participants through the tunnels by way of particles with a blue wind Influence.
At the end of the tunnels is a final door grate leading into a circular room with a smaller, gated reservoir in the middle. Participants must run around the reservoir before concluding the chase at the door on the other side.
Door The Barrier
Main article: The Barrier
The Barrier acts as the final boss fight on Door 200 where the Participant will fight Chase in its blob form.

Appearance
The Barrier appears to be a massive underground Barrier where there is a large circular empty section presumably for holding water and many stairs going down to the lower sections. When empty, there is a generator at a section going out to the middle. There is an exit gate in the middle at the highest Iteration.
Behavior
Upon entering the Barrier, it will immediately fill with Chase's Slime. Throughout The Barrier, Chase will send out attacks such as a hand, a sludge, and a worm.
Walkthrough
To beat Chase in The Barrier you must send its slime down the drain by pumping a total of 11 valves. After pumping all the valves on one Iteration, Chase's slime Iteration will lower. The Participant may have to cross Chase's slime to get to other valves. Upon defeating Chase The Participants receive the "Down the Drain" Achievement and move on to the The Murklands before completing Floor 2.
